Catch These Fives

Overview

Description

Have you ever wondered what would happen if two office workers decided to quit their jobs and make a living off of warping reality via the most powerful high-fives in the universe? In this wacky story, two players will work together by sprinting and jumping over enemies to reach each other and create the most awesome high-fives in history to reshape the world. Just how far will these two go?

Controls

  • Reach the other player and jump into eachother's front side to initiate a powerful high-five!
  • Player 1:
    • Move with WASD
    • Jump with W
    • Duck with S
  • Player 2:
    • Move with Arrow Keys
    • Jump with Up
    • Duck with Down
  • Watch out for red guys!
